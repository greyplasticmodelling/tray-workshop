Tray Workshop guide

Movement trays with magnet cutouts

Tray Workshop can add circular magnet recesses to movement tray STL files. Magnet cutouts can be used to help secure models in ranked units or support generated matching bases.

Open Tray Workshop

What you can adjust

  • Magnet diameter and recess depth.
  • Whether magnet cutouts are placed from the tray surface or from the underside.
  • Generated matching bases that can use the same magnet settings.

Always check your magnet dimensions with calipers and print a test piece before committing to a full unit tray.
