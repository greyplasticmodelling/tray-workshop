What this preset does

The preset uses a 100 x 150 mm target adapter tray with a 60 x 100 mm cutout. You can adjust height, tolerance, magnet options, floor options, and finishing options before downloading the STL file.

Unofficial fan-made utility. Warhammer, Warhammer The Old World, and Warhammer Fantasy Battle are referenced only to describe compatible tabletop base and movement tray use cases.
